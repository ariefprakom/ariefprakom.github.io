About me

My name is Arief Rachman. I am Pranata Komputer working in the government sector, specializing in the development of web applications and data processing systems. With experience in using Django Framework, I have successfully built applications that integrate with PDDIKTI data to generate critical analytics and visualization such as study success ratios and on-time graduation rates.

I am proficient in various programming languages including Python, HTML, and JavaScript, as well as database systems like PostgreSQL and MySQL. I am also adept at working with APIs and implementing search functionalities to enhance user experience. Additionally, I have experience in Docker, Git version control, and data visualization techniques.

Outside of work, I enjoy running to stay active and writing on Medium, where I share insights about technology, programming, and my professional journey. I look forward to collaborating with others to create impactful and efficient digital solutions. Thank you!
